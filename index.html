Policies & Terms

Clear Rules.
Zero Ambiguity.

We believe in full transparency. Here is exactly how we operate.

Communication Policy

No Direct Employee Contact

All communication, feedback, and issues must be routed through the designated FSA Manager. This protects both parties and maintains a clean, professional workflow.

Seat Policy

Bring Your Own System — $300/Seat/Month

If a company representative wishes to work from FSA Workspace on their own device, a dedicated seat is available at a flat $300/month. Covers desk, internet, utilities and on-site support.

Legal Policy

Agreement with Company — Not Employees

All contracts, agreements, SLAs and legal arrangements are signed exclusively between FSA Workspace and the client company. This ensures full accountability and a simplified legal relationship.

Holiday Policy

Pakistan Public Holidays — Workspace Closed

FSA Workspace observes all official Pakistani public holidays: 14th August, 9th & 10th Muharram (Ashura), Eid ul Fitr, and Eid ul Adha. The workspace will remain closed on these dates.
